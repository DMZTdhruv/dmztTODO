How to use it

  • 1. If you want to add a daily task, click on the "Add items here" text box and type your task. Then, click on the "+" icon to add your task to the list.

  • 2. If you have completed a task, simply click on the checkbox preceding the list item or click on the task itself to mark it as complete, and it will be displayed with a strike-through.
